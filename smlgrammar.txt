simple past                : subject + v2
simple present             : subject + v1 (+s/es for he/she/it)
simple future              : subject + will + v1
---
continuous past            : subject + was/were + v1 + ing
continuous present         : subject + am/is/are + v1 + ing
continuous future          : subject + will be + v1 + ing
---
perfect past               : subject + had + v3
perfect present            : subject + have/has + v3
perfect future             : subject + will have + v3
---
perfect continuous past    : subject + had been + v1 + ing
perfect continuous present : subject + have/has been + v1 + ing
perfect continuous future  : subject + will have been + v1 + ing
---
have (has for he/she/it)
---
was (were for we/you/they)
---
v1 verb = base form        : see
v2 verb = past form        : saw
v3 verb = past participle  : seen
